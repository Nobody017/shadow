Solo Leveling, also alternatively translated as Only I Level Up, is a South Korean
web novel written by Chugong. It was serialized in Kakao's digital comic
and fiction platform KakaoPage beginning on July 25, 2016, and was later published
in full by D&C Media under their Papyrus label on November 4, 2016.
